# 🏠 Smart PG & Mess Management System

A Smart PG & Mess Management System that automates attendance tracking, dynamic billing, payment verification, AI-powered food demand prediction, inventory management, and complaint handling.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Student Management
- Dynamic Meal Plans
- Attendance Tracking
- Automatic Billing
- Payment Management
- OCR Payment Verification
- AI Food Demand Prediction
- Inventory Management
- Complaint Management
- AI Chat Assistant
- Analytics Dashboard
